Analysis 
The solo student pilot reported that, during takeoff, the airplane started to veer left. He attempted to 
recover with right rudder input, but the airplane continued to veer left, departed the runway, and then 
came to rest in a ditch against the perimeter fence. 
The airplane sustained substantial damage to the left lift strut.
The student pilot reported that there were no preaccident mechanical failures or malfunctions with the 
airplane that would have precluded normal operation. 
Probable Cause and Findings
The National Transportation Safety Board determines the probable cause(s) of this accident to be:
The student pilot's failure to maintain directional control during takeoff.
