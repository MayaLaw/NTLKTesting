Analysis 
The pilot reported that, during landing on a private dirt airstrip on his ranch, the airplane collided with a 
cow. The pilot added that he did not see the cow during the approach or on the runway during 
touchdown.  
The airplane sustained substantial damage to the right wing and right engine mounts.
The pilot reported that there were no preaccident mechanical failures or malfunctions with the airplane 
that would have precluded normal operation.
Probable Cause and Findings
The National Transportation Safety Board determines the probable cause(s) of this accident to be:
The pilot's failure to see and avoid a cow on the airstrip during landing.
